Crowd elects new fighters when first two work it out

A large crowd eager to see some excitement gathered round what looked to be the beginnings of a fight last Thursday. Two boys were facing off at each other, their fists raised as though they were ready to ruin one another's backs for years to come. But before they did so (to their own relief, in hindsight) one of them realized he would absolutely lose quite badly and decided to sit down and talk about it. Eventually they reached an agreement that they would stagger their bathroom trips (and therefore not race, as was the problem) and walked away from the situation friends. Thankfully for all but the two about to be described, the crowd realized what was happening as soon as the original two opponents sat down and began a lottery to decide who would replace these two consiliatory snowflakes and provide the senseless entertainment promised to them by no one. Eventually two were decided, and they went up to continue the previously resolved brawl. At press time, they were creeping out the already stagnating crowd with how into it they were. It is currently suspected by Canyon Chronicles staff that the lottery may have been rigged.
